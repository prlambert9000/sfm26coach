# Exercise Guide — Chassis Build (Apr 20 – May 17)

Quick visual reference for every exercise in the 4-week rehab block. One canonical video per exercise plus a "what good looks like" and a "common mistake to avoid."

**(PT)** = prescribed directly by Akanksha A Sojitra, PT. Non-negotiable. Do at prescribed frequency and cues.

All other exercises are coach-added (supplemental) and consistent with PT goals. Re-review with PT at May 15 follow-up.

---

## Daily Mobility Flush (every day, ~12 min)

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Foam roller T-spine extensions **(PT)** | [Rehab Hero](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9Y11Kc0E0og) | Movement segment-by-segment through upper back only, head supported, ribs stay down. | Letting the lumbar spine extend or the neck fall backward instead of isolating the thoracic region. |
| Standing lunges with hip IR + extension **(PT)** | [Keep It Moving PT playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLU6ArKmdbFYJeIQUMfskXJeO8peVDowJ8) | Pelvis tucked, glutes squeeze to hold extension while stance leg rotates inward. | Arching the low back or letting the hip flex forward to fake internal rotation. |
| World's greatest stretch | [The Prehab Guys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j3cI3HK9nns) | Deep lunge with tall torso, elbow drives to instep, thoracic rotation opens clean to the ceiling. | Rushing through positions or collapsing the back knee. |
| 90/90 hip stretch (seated) | [Squat University](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VYvMMw8z3rE) | Front shin square to body, both sit bones down, long spine as you hinge forward. | Letting the front-leg hip lift off the ground — cheats the IR stretch. |
| Half-kneeling couch stretch | [Kelly Starrett](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/TML8Vqy-ACQ) | Back shin vertical against wall, glutes squeezed, ribs down, pelvis posteriorly tilted. | Arching the lumbar spine to fake depth instead of locking the pelvis. |
| Hip CARs | [Squat University / Kinetic Sports Rehab](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5kM-o61Z14I) | Slow, controlled full-ROM circles, body locked and stable via bracing. | Shifting the pelvis or low back instead of moving purely from the hip. |
| Cat-cow | [The Prehab Guys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vuyUwtHl694) | Segmental flexion/extension through whole spine, pelvis and head lead motion. | Rocking head/tail only without moving the intermediate segments. |

## Hip & Core A — Monday (IR + Posterior Chain)

### Warm-up

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Banded hip distraction (for IR) | [Squat University](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=99pb0NnE4Kw) | Band pulls femoral head laterally while you drive into end-range IR. | Letting the band slip too high on the thigh or not enough distraction tension. |
| 90/90 hip transitions | [Conor Harris](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ogU-azeGe_k) | Both knees stay in contact with the ground as you pivot, pelvis leading rotation. | Picking up the knees or hiking the hip to throw the leg across. |
| Banded glute bridges | [The Prehab Guys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hI_TzhcMOdI) | Heels drive down, glutes fire first, knees press out against band at top. | Overextending the low back or letting knees cave in (collapsing the band). |

### Main Circuit — 3 rounds

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| 90/90 PAILs/RAILs (hip IR isometric) | [FRC tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wNNILs6VA5U) | Ramp up to a hard isometric push into the floor, then contract rotators to lift out. | Skipping the progressive ramp or using explosive motion instead of slow isometric. |
| Side-lying clamshell (banded) | [Kinetic Sports Rehab](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EG5_gXcfozw) | Heels stacked, pelvis perpendicular to floor, knee opens purely from glute contraction. | Rolling the pelvis backward to get more "range" and letting the low back do the work. |
| Single-leg glute bridge | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qwilebp_3BY) | Hips stay level, drive through heel of working leg, ribs down at top. | Non-working hip dropping toward the floor or arching the low back to push higher. |
| Hip airplane | [Squat University](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9svtEV4vkp0) | Hinge forward on stance leg, rotate pelvis IR and ER while shoulders and back leg stay in one line. | Losing the hinge and rotating from the upper body instead of the stance hip. |
| Copenhagen plank | [E3 Rehab](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YRRnnZsRs9U) | Straight line head-to-feet (also from top-down view), adductor does the lifting. | Letting hips sag or using the top hand to push up instead of the adductor. |
| Dead bug — slow march with banded overhead pulldown **(PT)** | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oiD9Yv0CMQ) | Low back stays flat-pressed to floor the entire time, opposite arm/leg slow and controlled. | Letting the lumbar spine arch off the floor when the limbs extend. |
| Chin tuck + lifts, front and diagonal **(PT)** | [Rehab Hero](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ojeZxd2gwsI) | Gentle nod first (chin to Adam's apple), then a small lift keeping forehead parallel to floor. | Jutting the chin forward or using SCM/big superficial muscles instead of deep flexors. |
| Pallof press isometric hold | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_kLiyBpsRaQ) | Ribs stacked over pelvis, arms fully extended, zero trunk rotation for the hold duration. | Letting the band pull you into rotation or shrugging shoulders up. |

### Finisher

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Wall sit | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pjbGm5EQd1s) | Back flat on wall, knees at 90°, knees stay behind toes, weight through heels. | Knees drifting over toes, or sliding higher as you fatigue. |
| Terminal knee extension (TKE) with band — right leg | [Physio REHAB (Tim Keeley)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nfJ5QCx_fSg) | Quad contracts hard to drive the last 10–15° of knee extension against band resistance. | Hyperextending/locking the knee backward or using the whole body to push against the band. |

### Cool-down

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Pigeon pose | [San Diego Chiropractic](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2vwIdJbOqx4) | Hips square, back leg long and straight, prop under front glute if needed to level pelvis. | Collapsing onto the side of the bent-leg hip or letting the back leg bend/flop out. |

## Hip & Core B — Friday (Hip Strength + Anti-Rotation)

### Warm-up

Foam roll + Quadruped banded hip IR (PT) + 90/90 PAILs/RAILs (see Hip & Core A above), plus:

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Cossack squat | [Squat University](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tpczTeSkHz0) | Loaded heel stays down, working-side toe can lift, chest tall as you sink into deep lateral squat. | Rounding the back or letting the working-side knee collapse inward. |
| Fire hydrants | [The Prehab Guys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qPdR-PeU7Mc) | Knee drives straight out to the side, hips square to floor, no trunk rotation. | Rotating the whole torso to lift the leg higher or arching the lumbar spine. |

### Main Circuit — 3 rounds

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Bulgarian split squat | [Squat University](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-4LVK1crLSw) | Torso leans slightly forward, front shin stacks over midfoot, slow controlled eccentric down. | Front knee caving in or staying too upright (loads the knee instead of the hip). |
| Single-leg RDL | [The Prehab Guys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qRcMROB1l_E) | Hinge at hip with long spine, hips stay square to floor, back leg extends as counterweight. | Rotating the pelvis open or bending the stance knee like a squat instead of hinging. |
| Side plank with hip abduction — **toe pointing to floor (PT cue)** | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t6aCHdkyoy0) | Hips stacked and lifted, top **toe points DOWN** (IR), leg lifts from glute med not TFL. | Pointing toes up/forward — shifts work to TFL and hip flexors instead of glute med. |
| Banded lateral / monster walks | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=42cYOwpwOIc) | Athletic quarter-squat stance, constant band tension, short controlled steps with level hips. | Standing too tall or letting trailing leg snap back (loses glute tension). |
| Bird dog | [The Prehab Guys](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-LRjkbEy-qU) | Opposite arm and leg extend to one straight line, hips and shoulders square to the floor. | Arching the low back, rotating the pelvis, or shrugging the shoulder to raise the arm. |
| Dead bug — slow march with banded pulldown **(PT)** | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6oiD9Yv0CMQ) | Same as Hip & Core A. | Same as Hip & Core A. |
| Chin tuck + lifts **(PT)** | [Rehab Hero](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ojeZxd2gwsI) | Same as Hip & Core A. | Same as Hip & Core A. |
| Pallof press with rotation | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RQJ-9NgZXYs) | Controlled rotation driven from the trunk with ribs over pelvis, no hip sway. | Letting the hips swing with the rotation instead of resisting through the core. |
| Front plank with shoulder tap | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QOCn3_iOAro) | Hips perfectly level as hand lifts, feet can widen for stability, core braced. | Hips rocking side to side or dropping as you tap — that's a core failure. |

### Finisher

Wall sit + TKE right leg (see Hip & Core A).

### Cool-down

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Pigeon pose | [San Diego Chiropractic](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2vwIdJbOqx4) | See Hip & Core A. | See Hip & Core A. |
| Quadruped adductor rock-back | [Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yF8o6I6aSZg) | One leg extends straight out to the side, neutral spine, rock hips back toward the heel. | Rounding the low back or letting the stretched leg's knee bend. |

## Upper Push (Mon, Fri)

Pure maintenance — don't chase PRs.

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Dumbbell bench press | [Athlean-X](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SHsUIZiNdeY) | Shoulder blades retracted/down, elbows at ~45°, dumbbells track over lower chest. | Flaring elbows to 90° and pressing over face/shoulders (trashes the shoulder). |
| Dumbbell overhead press | [Jeff Nippard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_RlRDWO2jfg) | Press in scapular plane, ribs down, full lockout overhead without arching low back. | Hyperextending the low back and leaning back to complete the press. |
| Push-ups | [Athlean-X](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-Mbr55h3BeQ) | Full ROM chest to floor, straight line head-to-heels, elbows ~45° from torso. | Sagging hips, flared elbows, or head poking forward. |
| Dumbbell lateral raise | [Jeff Nippard](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/NZsldrqqca8) | Slight forward lean, arms slightly bent, reach out to the walls with elbows higher than wrists. | Shrugging traps up or swinging momentum to raise the weights. |
| Tricep overhead extension | [Jeff Nippard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=popGXI-qs98) | Elbows stay narrow and pointed up, full stretch overhead, lockout with tricep contraction. | Elbows flaring out wide or cutting the ROM short at the stretch. |

## Upper Pull (Wed, Sat)

| Exercise | Video | What good looks like | Common mistake |
|----------|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Pull-ups | [Athlean-X](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sIvJTfGxdFo) | Grip just outside shoulders, shoulder blades pull down first, chest rises to bar. | Kipping/swinging, or starting with the shoulders shrugged up. |
| Lat pulldown | [Jeff Nippard](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/hnSqbBk15tw) | Medium overhand grip (~1–1.5x shoulder width), drive elbows down-and-back, slight back lean. | Pulling with arms only or going too wide (kills lat ROM). |
| Single-arm dumbbell row | [Athlean-X](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gfUg6qWohTk) | Flat back, dumbbell travels to hip (not armpit), elbow drives back led by the shoulder blade. | Rotating the torso to heave the weight up (cheat reps from the trunk). |
| Face pulls | [Athlean-X](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ljgqer1ZpXg) | Thumbs back/palms out, elbows at or below shoulder height, external rotation at the end. | Pulling with elbows too high — turns it into an upright row for the traps. |
| Dumbbell bicep curls | [Jeff Nippard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i1YgFZB6alI) | Elbow stays pinned at the side, full supination at the top, controlled eccentric, no swing. | Swinging the torso or letting elbows drift forward. |
| Band pull-aparts | [Athlean-X](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yIVdFq14LiE) | Thumbs up/palms out, lead with the backs of the hands, squeeze shoulder blades together. | Gripping overhand and cranking with traps instead of rear delts. |
